Privacy Policy — No Internet

Last updated: July 3, 2026

No Internet is a Chrome extension that shows a customizable offline page with the dino game when your connection fails.

Data we collect

We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal information. The extension does not use analytics or third-party tracking.

Data stored on your device

The extension stores the following locally in your browser only:

  • Theme preferences and custom theme images you add
  • Dino game high score

This data stays on your device via Chrome’s storage API and localStorage. It is not sent to any server operated by the developer.

Network access

The extension intercepts network errors in your browser to show the offline page. It does not read page content, passwords, or browsing history for any other purpose.

If you click an external link (for example, a support link), that opens in a new tab and is handled by your browser normally.

Permissions

  • webNavigation — detect when a page fails to load due to a network error
  • storage — save your theme and score preferences locally
  • host access — network errors can occur on any website you visit

Children

This extension is not directed at children under 13.

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected on this page.
